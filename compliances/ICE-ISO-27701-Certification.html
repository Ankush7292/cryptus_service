    • ISO / ICE 27701 Certification

    ISO/IEC 27701 is a certification that extends ISO/IEC 27001 to include requirements for Privacy Information Management Systems (PIMS), helping organizations manage privacy risks and comply with regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

    data privacy is of paramount importance, achieving ISO/IEC 27701 certification demonstrates your commitment to protecting personal information. Our certification services help organizations implement and maintain a Privacy Information Management System (PIMS) that aligns with global privacy standards, ensuring compliance and enhancing trust with customers and stakeholders.

    Why ISO/IEC 27701 Certification Matters

    ISO/IEC 27701 is an extension to the ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002 standards, specifically focused on privacy management. It provides guidelines for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continuously improving a PIMS. Achieving ISO/IEC 27701 certification helps organizations:

  • Demonstrate compliance with data protection regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws.
  • Enhance customer trust by showcasing a commitment to privacy and data protection.
  • Mitigate risks associated with data breaches and privacy violations.
  • Improve internal processes related to personal data handling and protection.
    • Our ISO/IEC 27701 Certification Services

    Our comprehensive ISO/IEC 27701 Certification services guide you through every step of the certification process. We employ a systematic approach to ensure your organization meets the stringent requirements of the standard.

    Key Components of Our ISO/IEC 27701 Certification Services

    1. Gap Analysis

  • Initial Assessment : We conduct a thorough assessment of your current privacy practices and controls to identify gaps and areas for improvement.
  • Detailed Report : Our gap analysis report provides a detailed overview of your organization’s compliance status, highlighting deficiencies and recommending corrective actions.

    • 2. Implementation Support

  • PIMS Framework Development : We assist in developing a robust PIMS framework tailored to your organization’s specific needs and aligned with ISO/IEC 27701 requirements.
  • Policy and Procedure Documentation : Our experts help you create and document policies, procedures, and controls necessary for effective privacy management.
  • Training and Awareness : We provide training sessions for your staff to ensure they understand their roles and responsibilities in maintaining privacy and data protection.

    • 3. Internal Audit

  • Pre-Certification Audit : We conduct an internal audit to evaluate the effectiveness of your PIMS and ensure it meets ISO/IEC 27701 standards.
  • Audit Report : Our audit report includes findings, non-conformities, and recommendations for remediation to prepare you for the certification audit.

    • 4. Certification Audit Support

  • Audit Coordination : We assist in coordinating with the certification body and preparing for the external certification audit.
  • Audit Representation : Our team provides support during the audit, helping to address any queries and ensure a smooth certification process.
  • Post-Audit Support : We help you address any non-conformities identified during the audit and implement corrective actions promptly.

    • 5. Continuous Improvement and Maintenance

  • Ongoing Compliance : We offer continuous monitoring and support to ensure your PIMS remains compliant with ISO/IEC 27701 standards.
  • Periodic Audits : Our periodic internal audits help you maintain certification and adapt to any changes in privacy regulations or organizational processes.
  • Consultation and Updates : We provide ongoing consultation to help you stay ahead of emerging privacy threats and regulatory changes.
