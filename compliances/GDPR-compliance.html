    • GDPR Complaince

    Our company ensures GDPR compliance, safeguarding personal data through transparent practices and respecting individual privacy rights as mandated by European regulations.

    We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. Our compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) ensures that your data is handled securely and transparently.

    What is GDPR?

    The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a regulation by the European Union that strengthens and unifies data protection for all individuals within the EU. It also addresses the export of personal data outside the EU. GDPR aims to give individuals control over their personal data and simplify the regulatory environment for international business.

    Our Commitment to GDPR Compliance

    We are committed to ensuring the security and confidentiality of your personal data. This page outlines how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your information in compliance with GDPR.

    Key Components of Our GDPR Compliance

    1. Data Collection and Use

  • Types of Data Collected : We collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, and other relevant information necessary for providing our services.
  • Purpose of Data : Collection: We collect your data to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, process transactions, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Legal Basis for Processing : We process your personal data based on your consent, the performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate interests.

    • 2. Data Subject Rights

    Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access : You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification : You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure : You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability : You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and transmit it to another data controller.
  • Right to Object : You can object to the processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

    • 3. Data Security

    We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data, including.

  • Encryption : We use encryption to protect personal data in transit and at rest.
  • Access Controls : We implement strict access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel have access to personal data.
  • Security Monitoring : We continuously monitor our systems for potential vulnerabilities and threats.

    • 4. Data Breach Notification

    In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have established procedures to:

  • Notify Authorities : We will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of a data breach, if required by GDPR.
  • Inform Affected Individuals : If the breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will inform you without undue delay.

    • 5. Data Transfers

    We may transfer your personal data to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) to provide our services. When we do so, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses : We use standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.
  • Privacy Shield Framework : For transfers to the United States, we ensure compliance with the Privacy Shield Framework.

    • 6. Data Retention

    We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Providing Services : Retaining data for the duration of our relationship to provide and support our services.
  • Legal Obligations : Retaining data as required by law, such as tax and accounting regulations.
  • Legitimate Interests : Retaining data as necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as fraud prevention and security.
